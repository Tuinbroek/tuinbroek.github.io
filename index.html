About

Myself

Hey there! My name is Richie Elia and I am a 24 year old UX Design student at the The Hague University of Applied Sciences set to graduate in 2022. Prior to my studies I have worked as a Head Chef for "Brouwerij de Prael", a multi-franchise brewery with locations throughout the Netherlands. Besides working there I have worked at many other hospitality businesses and I have filled pretty much any role available in hospitaly. This is one of the reasons I decided to start the User Experience Design course at THUAS, after which I hope to tackle a new adventure in the field of UX Design. In my free time I enjoy making and listening to music. In addition to that, I also enjoy cooking, playing video games and socializing with friends.

What I can do for you

Front-End Development

I am proficient in all programmes in the Adobe Creative Cloud. Besides that I am also proficient at using Javascript, HTML and CSS. If you need your front-end done, I am your guy! ;)

(Graphic) Design

As mentioned before, I am proficient in all programmes in the Adobe Creative Cloud. Therefore, I can help you by designing your logo, business cards, flyers and much more! Besides digital design I am also skilled in making physical prototypes and/or visualisations.
